Kathleen Koch

  • Kathleen Koch is an Irish-American mixed-media artist, poet, singer-songwriter and social healer, currently living in France, just outside Geneva, Switzerland. She has studied art with Pascale Lorge (Brussels), Alain Gegout (Brétigny) and Nicole Henke (Divonne-les-Bains). She has organized art exhibits and offered art therapy courses in Geneva. Her paintings have been privately commissioned and displayed in various homes around the world. Kathleen has studied in five countries and holds two Master’s degrees. Brought up as an Irish girl in Wisconsin, she married a German and raised three children (all of whom are now older than she is). Kathleen’s poems and songs include tributes to beloved places and people (as well as deceased pets!), and focus on a variety of social, psychological, and environmental issues. Her poems have appeared in several local publications, and many more are shared on her Facebook page, Facebook Profile . Many of her songs can be found on YouTube Channel .
